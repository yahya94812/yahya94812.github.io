# Starting My Bachelor of Engineering (CS) at PDA College 🚀

I've finally landed at **PDA College of Engineering**, and I'm starting this journey with a clear vision and a lot of excitement for what's ahead.

My goal by the end of these 4 years? Build a computer system completely from scratch — hardware and software — as proof of everything this degree has taught me. I've already started learning programming, and honestly, it's been fun and exciting so far.

Today was my first day of college, and it kicked off with my first Maths class, followed by the Maths lab — which, interestingly, used Python. Since I was already familiar with it, I took the chance to have a little fun: I tweaked the title string of a matplotlib graph just to impress the lab faculty. Small win, but it felt good.

I also made my first friends on campus today. Overall, it's been a great start — and I'm heading into this journey with a lot of confidence.
